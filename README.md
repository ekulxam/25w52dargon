# 25w52dargon - Dragonshot

A stronger dragon mod made for Fabric Holiday 2025
Features:
- Dragon now reflects projectiles while sitting at podium
- Projectiles owned by the dragon cannot hit the dragon or its parts
- Dragon breath deals increased damage
- Dragon now shoots more fireballs per strafe
- Crystals now have a bossbar
- Dragon damage now ignores armor and cooldown
- Dragon is now immune to bed explosions
- All crystals within range now contribute to the dragon's regeneration
- Fix MC-272431 ([bugs.mojang.com](https://bugs.mojang.com/browse/MC/issues/MC-272431) | [mojira.dev](https://mojira.dev/MC-272431))
- Dragon body knockback and damage no longer apply to endermen
